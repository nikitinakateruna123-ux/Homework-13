My Favorite Pet

Betta Fish

The Betta Fish is one of my favorite aquarium fish. It is famous for its bright colors and beautiful fins. Betta fish are easy to care for and can live in small aquariums with proper conditions.

Interesting Facts

  • Betta fish can breathe air from the surface.
  • They come in many colors.
  • Their average lifespan is 3–5 years.
  • They are popular among aquarium enthusiasts.
